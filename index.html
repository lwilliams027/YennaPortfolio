About the Artist

Every painting starts with silence

Yelena is a young painter who works entirely by hand. From city crowds to Sedona landscapes, classical still lifes to stormy abstracts — her range is matched only by her dedication to the physical medium.

In an age of screens, every brushstroke in Yelena's work is intentional. Every canvas holds real texture you can feel. Nothing is digital. Everything is real.
