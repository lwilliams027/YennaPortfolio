About the Artist

Every painting starts with inspiration

Yelena is from a small town in Michigan, and she spends her days creating art at home. From hyper-realistic paintings, classic still lifes, to custom-painted shoes, she loves trying out new styles and challenging herself to do more.

In an age of screens and AI at your fingertips, every brush stroke in Yelena's work is real. Every art piece holds years of dedication and practice.
